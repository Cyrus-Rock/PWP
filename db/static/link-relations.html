Link Relations

This page explains the link relations of the whole API.

The following is the list and explanations of the entry points:

  • seat-all: retreives all the information for the seats.
  • plane-all: retreives all the information for the planes.
  • flight-all: retreives all the information for the flights.
  • offer-all: retreives all the information for the offers.
  • client-all: retreives all the information for the clients.

The following is the list and explanations of the `/clients/{client}/` resource:

  • delete-client: deletes the client of interest.
  • edit-client: edits the client of interest.
  • up: goes up to its parent resource `/clients/`.

The following is the list and explanations of the `/clients/` resource:

  • add-client: adds a new client to the database.
  • self: goes to the resource `/clients/{client}/` for this specific client

The following is the list and explanations of the `/planes/{plane_id}/` resource:

  • delete-plane: deletes the plane of interest.
  • edit-plane: edits the plane of interest.
  • up: goes up to its parent resource `/planes/`.

The following is the list and explanations of the `/planes/` resource:

  • add-plane: adds a new plane to the database.
  • self: goes to the resource `/planes/{plane_id}/` for this specific plane

The following is the list and explanations of the `/seats/{plane_id}/` resource:

  • delete-seat: deletes the seat information for the provided plane's ID.
  • edit-seat: edits the seat information for the provided plane's ID.
  • up: goes up to its parent resource `/seats/`.

The following is the list and explanations of the `/seats/` resource:

  • add-seat: adds a new seat information to the database.
  • self: goes to the resource `/seats/{plane_id}/` for this specific seat.

The following is the list and explanations of the `/flights/{origin}/{destination}/` resource:

  • up: goes up to its parent resource `/flights/`.

The following is the list and explanations of the `/flights/` resource:

  • add-flights: adds a list of flights to the database.
  • self: goes to the resource `/flights/{origin}/{destination}/` for this specific flight.

The following is the list and explanations of the `/offers/{client_token}/{origin}/{destination}/`

  • up: goes up to its parent resource `/offers/`.

The following is the list and explanations of the `/offers/` resource:

  • add-offer: adds a list of offers to the database.
  • self: goes to the resource `/offers/{client_token}/{origin}/{destination}/` for this specific offer.

Namespaces

The name space alden is prepended before any of the provided links. For example, edit-plane becomes alden:edit-plane or flight-all becomes alden:flight-all.
